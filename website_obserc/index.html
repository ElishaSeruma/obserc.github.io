FAQ

You’re Not Alone in Figuring This Out.
Here’s What You Might Be Wondering.

What is Obserc, and how does it work?

Obserc is a smart safety companion that uses biometric signals, emotional patterns, and physical context to detect distress. When something seems wrong, it notifies your Circle and can escalate based on severity using a colour coded system from Green to Black.

Can I customize who gets notified?

Yes. You create your Circle. These are the people Obserc reaches out to when your body signals distress. You can update your Circle at any time — even set different groups for different environments.

What if I trigger it by mistake?

You’ll always have a few quiet seconds to cancel before anything is sent. Obserc gives you calm control first — no sudden calls or flashing lights.

How is my data handled?

Your data is encrypted and, when possible, processed directly on your device. Obserc doesn’t sell or mine your personal moments — you choose what gets shared and when.

Will this work if I don’t have Wi-Fi or signal?

Yes. Obserc is designed with fallback logic. Your device and app still communicate, and your Circle gets notified through available channels. Even with weak connectivity, you’re never fully alone.
